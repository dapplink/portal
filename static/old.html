Dapplink technology is a way to build and run websites, applications and services directly on the blockchain without having to have and maintain servers, databases and storage systems. Your applications and data will be kept and available forever without any additional costs. Anyone may get access to your digital products using a web browser or via http request. One may consider dapplink as extension of the World Wide Web to the blockchain area.

All resources in the Dapplink infrastructure are represented as NFT, so any kind of your work (website, application, art) become your own NFT-token at the moment of its creation. You may publish your works in the dapplink destribution service where users may see them. Also dapplink provide Dapplink Coin (DLC) for internal payments and full-featured economical ecosystem where users, developers and creators can mutually beneficial cooperate with each other.
